My Story

Being in love with computers and technology from an early age and never having the opportunity to follow through with my passion due to lack of support, was worst feeling in the world. A childhood dream of working with computers being cut short without ever even being given a chance is something that stuck with me through my entire life, and I have finally decided to give it my all in order to make my dream a reality. Being a Chef for the last 10 years has been amazing. Everything it has taught me along the way such as perserverance, hard work, leadership, people skills, creativity, organizational skills, and time management will surely translate really well as I transition into this new and exciting world of software development. I get excited every day just thinking about the new technologies I am going to learn or practice that day, and I am looking forward to bringing all that passion and willingness while I dive into this new and exciting industry.
